# EMESIS - User Guide

Welcome to **EMESIS**, a safe, AI-moderated confession space where you can share your thoughts anonymously or with your profile.

## Getting Started

### 1. Authentication
- **Google Login**: Quick access using your Google account.
- **Email Login**: Traditional email/password registration.
- **Anonymous Mode**: Browse and post without an account (limited features).

### 2. Creating a Confession
- Go to the **Feed** page.
- Type your thought in the "What's on your mind?" box.
- Use **#hashtags** to categorize your post.
- Toggle the **Anonymous** switch if you want to hide your identity.
- Your location is automatically detected to show local confessions (can be disabled in browser settings).
- **AI Moderation**: Every post is analyzed by Gemini AI. Content that violates community guidelines will be blocked.

## Social Features

### 1. Feed & Interaction
- **Like**: Heart a post to show support.
- **Comment**: Reply to posts. You can use **Markdown** (e.g., `**bold**`, `*italic*`, `` `code` ``) and **@mentions**.
- **Share**: Generate a beautiful sharing card for Instagram Stories or WhatsApp, or copy a direct link.

### 2. Stories
- View 24-hour stories at the top of your feed.
- Create your own **Text** or **Image** stories.
- **Auto-Advance**: Stories play automatically. Press and hold to pause.

### 3. Explore & Discovery
- Find **Trending Topics** based on popular hashtags.
- Discover **Suggested Users** to follow.
- Search for specific usernames or tags.

### 4. Profiles
- Customize your **Bio** and **Accent Color**.
- Track your **Followers** and **Following**.
- View your history of confessions.

### 5. Notifications
- Stay updated on likes, comments, and new followers in real-time.

## Safety & Moderation
- **Reporting**: You can report inappropriate content via the post menu.
- **AI Guard**: Our system automatically filters unsafe content.
- **Blocking**: Block users in private chats to prevent further contact.
